About Us


OUR MISSION

Our mission is to meet customer requirements with diverse products, and make their production and packaging in high quality and according to international standards.
We strive for excellence in all segments of production and are creative in various food recipes.

OUR VISION

Our vision is to become a leading company in the production and packaging of various high quality food products in Kosovo and beyond.
Also, to manage to penetrate with our products in the regional and international market.



ABOUT US

HAJÉ LLC is one of the leading companies in the production and packaging of many food products in Kosovo.

HAJÉ LLC, has inherited a long experience in quality and organization since 1996. HAJÉ at the end of 2020 has started with a new spirit, inheriting a range of products, and assets, and at the same time bringing a new energy with which aims to reorganize and rebuild genuine production policies in accordance with the requirements of consumers and the market in general.

HAJÉ products are diverse and cover several areas such as; nuts (raw peanuts, white peanuts, popcorn), pumpkin and sunflower seeds with different flavors, mashed potatoes in many varieties, choco-cream block in various varieties, and chocolate cream.

HAJÉ products have already been proven to have high quality, taste and nutritional values.

In addition to the production and packaging of a range of products, now, HAJÉ has cooperation with various supermarket chains within the country for the production and packaging of various products known as "Private Label" in accordance with their requirements.

On the other hand, within the supply chain with products for production and packaging, HAJÉ is also distinguished by a large range of local and international partners. HAJÉ is a leader in this segment thanks to her constructive, principled and very serious approach. HAJÉ partners are from different countries such as; Germany, Austria, Hungary, Slovenia, Czech Republic, Netherlands, Ukraine, Northern Macedonia, India, China, Argentina, Brazil, USA, etc ..

Thanks to the hard work and dedication we get from all our employees, now the products of HAJÉ are easily accessible to buy and can be found in supermarkets throughout Kosovo, but also abroad.
